Pretzel and Friends

Building a More Just and Peaceful World

Our organization contributes to achieving SDG 16: Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions by promoting peaceful communities, improving access to justice, and strengthening accountable institutions. We facilitate peacebuilding workshops, community dialogues, and conflict-resolution training to reduce tensions and support social cohesion. To ensure justice for all, we provide legal assistance, conduct rights-awareness campaigns, and help vulnerable individuals navigate legal processes. We also work closely with local institutions by offering capacity-building programs that enhance transparency, ethical leadership, and anti-corruption practices. In addition, we encourage civic participation by creating platforms for community involvement in decision-making and policy discussions. Through these combined efforts, our organization actively supports the development of peaceful, just, and inclusive societies.

Justice & Equality

Our organization promotes justice by offering legal aid services that support individuals who cannot afford representation, ensuring they receive fair treatment within the legal system. We conduct rights-awareness campaigns that educate communities about their legal protections and empower them to speak up against injustice. Through our human rights advocacy efforts, we work to protect vulnerable groups from discrimination, abuse, and exploitation by engaging with local authorities, monitoring violations, and pushing for policy reforms. We also collaborate with justice institutions to help simplify legal processes, making them more accessible, transparent, and inclusive. By removing barriers and amplifying marginalized voices, we ensure equal access to justice and help build a system that upholds fairness and human dignity for all.
